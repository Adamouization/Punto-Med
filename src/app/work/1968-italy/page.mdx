## Overview

In 1968, the distinguished group to which Punto Med belongs established its roots in Italy. 

This milestone marked the foundation of Gazeco S.A., a cutting-edge gas cooker manufacturing facility in Padova, and Deltex S.A., a pioneering producer of refrigerators and air conditioners in Corsico, Milan. 

These ventures set the stage for a tradition of excellence and innovation that continues to define our legacy.
